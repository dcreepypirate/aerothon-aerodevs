Welcome.

Welcome to AeroDev, the groundbreaking platform revolutionizing aircraft recycling for a sustainable future.

We are dedicated to connecting aircraft manufacturers, airlines, and recycling facilities to transform the end-of-life aircraft components into valuable resources, promoting environmental stewardship and circular economy practices in the aviation industry.

At AeroDev, we recognize the urgent need to address the environmental impact of retiring aircraft and the vast potential for repurposing and recycling their components. Our platform serves as a catalyst for change, fostering collaboration and streamlining the process of giving retired aircraft a new life.

Component Inventory Management

We provide manufacturers with a comprehensive system to manage their inventory of retired aircraft components.

Component Request System

Airlines can utilize our platform to browse the available inventory of retired aircraft components and submit requests for specific parts they require for maintenance or upgrades.

Analytics and Insights

Our data analytics provide valuable insights and trends related to the repurposing and recycling of aircraft components.
